Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, actions taken).

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history, summaries, and breakdowns.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues in the app.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, or deletion. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services to support its operation (e.g., hosting providers). These providers are given access only to the data necessary to perform their functions and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains no personal information and is cleared when you log out or close your browser. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.
